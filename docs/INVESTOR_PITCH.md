## üìä The Problem (30 seconds)

**"Let me tell you about a $3 billion blind spot in youth sports."**

- **600,000+ high school track athletes** in the US alone have no dedicated platform
- Athletes track PRs in **spreadsheets and notes apps**
- Coaches manage rosters via **group texts and paper sign-ups**
- College scouts spend **hundreds of hours** manually searching for talent
- Parents have **no visibility** into their athlete's progress

**The current solution?** Fragmented tools that don't talk to each other. MileSplit for results. Strava for runs. Instagram for highlights. Nothing connects the ecosystem.

---

## üí° The Solution (60 seconds)

**"TrackVerse is the all-in-one platform that connects the entire track & field ecosystem."**

### For Athletes:
- **Automatic tier rankings** - Log a time, instantly see where you rank nationally
- **Social feed** - Celebrate PRs, share workouts, build your brand
- **Recruiting profile** - Auto-generated from your performance data
- **Training hub** - Workouts, progress tracking, streak gamification

### For Coaches:
- **Google Classroom for track** - Assign workouts, track completion, analyze team performance
- **Roster management** - One code to join, instant team analytics
- **Meet management** - Registration, heat sheets, live results

### For Scouts:
- **Talent search engine** - Filter by event, time, location, grad year
- **Prospect lists** - Save and organize athletes
- **Direct messaging** - Compliant recruiting communication

## ‚ùì Anticipated Q&A

**Q: How do you acquire users?**
A: Coach-led viral loops. One coach signs up, invites 40 athletes. We target track coaches through state associations and coaching conferences.

**Q: What about MileSplit?**
A: MileSplit is a results database. We're a social platform. They're the record book; we're the daily experience.

**Q: How do you verify times?**
A: Coach verification, meet result imports, and community flagging. Fraudulent times get caught quickly in a competitive community.

**Q: Why mobile-first?**
A: Athletes are on their phones at the track. They want to log a PR immediately, not wait until they get home.

**Q: What's your CAC?**
A: Near-zero for athletes (viral). ~$50 for coaches (targeted ads + conferences). Scouts find us organically.

**Q: How defensible is this?**
A: Network effects + data moat. Once a team is on TrackVerse, switching costs are high. Our ranking algorithm improves with more data.
